Wine Tasting App

A mobile application designed to help wine enthusiasts discover, taste, and track their wine journey with expert guidance and community insights.

Mobile App Design User Research Prototyping React Development

Client

Personal Project

Timeline

8 weeks

Role

UX Designer, Developer

Tools

Figma, React, CSS

The Challenge

Wine appreciation is often intimidating for newcomers and even experienced enthusiasts struggle to track their tasting journey effectively. Existing wine apps focus primarily on purchasing or basic rating systems, leaving a gap for an educational, community-driven platform that guides users through their wine discovery process while making wine education accessible and enjoyable.

Research & Discovery

User Interviews

I conducted 12 in-depth interviews with wine enthusiasts ranging from beginners to sommeliers to understand their pain points and motivations.

8 casual wine drinkers
3 wine enthusiasts
1 certified sommelier

Key Insights

"I never remember what I liked"
Users struggle to recall wines they enjoyed, leading to repeated disappointing purchases.
"Wine vocabulary is intimidating"
Complex terminology creates barriers for beginners wanting to learn about wine.
"I want to discover, not just rate"
Users desire guided discovery experiences rather than simple rating systems.

User Personas

AS
Alex - The Curious Beginner
Age 28, Marketing Professional
"I enjoy wine but feel overwhelmed by all the choices. I want to learn but don't know where to start."
Goals: Learn wine basics, discover preferences
Frustrations: Intimidating terminology, analysis paralysis
MR
Maria - The Explorer
Age 35, Wine Enthusiast
"I love trying new wines but struggle to remember details about ones I've enjoyed."
Goals: Track tastings, discover new wines
Frustrations: Poor memory for wine details, scattered notes

Impact & Outcomes

The wine tasting app successfully addresses the key pain points identified in user research, creating an approachable entry point for wine education while providing value for experienced enthusiasts.

✓

Reduced Learning Barriers

Guided tasting flows made wine education accessible for beginners

✓

Improved Wine Discovery

Contextual recommendations helped users explore new wines confidently

✓

Enhanced Memory & Tracking

Smart journal helped users remember and rediscover favorite wines

Key Learnings

Progressive Disclosure is Powerful

Breaking complex wine knowledge into digestible steps made learning feel achievable rather than overwhelming.

Context Drives Confidence

Users needed to understand 'why' behind recommendations to trust the system and feel confident in their choices.

Personal Connection Matters

The most successful features were those that helped users connect their personal experiences to wine knowledge.
